Ams Ambassador - Most Wanted Class 2023

  • Student Ambassador of Engagement—Most Wanted Class 2023

Ams Ambassador, part of our school's arts showcase NHAT, runs alongside the Most Wanted Class competition. 60 contestants from 13 Specialized Classes compete through four rounds (Application, Interview, Top 20—Talent Round, Top 10—Traditional and Evening gowns). I was named Student Ambassador of Engagement, contributing to my class winning the Most Wanted Class title in the Grand Finale Night, the event's highest honor.

Charity Week

One of the most memorable and meaningful experiences I gained from the competition was during Charity Week, when all the students at my school collected old clothes and books to donate. The Ambassadors will personally deliver the gifts to children in need. This year, we represented the students of Hanoi-Amsterdam by donating recyclable paper and books, building a new bookcase, and repainting the deteriorated walls of the Special Needs Children's Area in Ba Vi.
